# Naiad
A USB-C rechargeable keychain (that hangs on your water bottle) that flashes to remind you to drink water if it hasn’t detected a sip in X minutes.

### What is Naiad?

Naiad is basically a small keychain type thing that hangs off my HUGEE waterbottle and will remind me to drink water when It hasn't detected a sip every X amount of minutes (in my case I chose 15 minutes). I made it because I have a really bad habit of going the entire day without drinking much water, and thought this was a fun way to solve it :)
